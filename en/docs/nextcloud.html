Nextcloud documentation

Discover everything behind ou Nextcloud offers.

A complete plateform

NextCloud is a rich service, storage and sharing platform. You can synchronize your files in the same way as Dropbox, Google Drive or iCloud, edit them collaboratively in the way of Google Docs, make videoconferences like Skype, manage your calendars as Google Calendar and synchronize your contacts identically than Google Contacts. There are also many other features available, including an RSS feed reader, bookmark manager, task manager and even e-mail client!

Private account on shared server

The account on a shared instance allows a single user (possibly a few) to benefit from all the features of Nextcloud at a lower cost.

Offers matching your needs

Choose from our proposals: 10 or 30 GB of storage, over 1, 3 or 12 months. If these are not suitable for you, send us a message from our contact form detailing your needs, We will offer you a totally personalized offer at an attractive price.

Two options, to choose from

We offer two different types of accounts: the first allows the encryption of your data on our server, the second allows access to the Collabora Online (CODE) software suite. Unfortunately these two options are not (yet) compatible, due to a technical limitation the Collabora server can't read the encrypted data from the Nextcloud server.

A modular unit

On request, we modify the available storage space on your account. If you want to reduce it we will refund the difference of what you have already paid. If you wish to increase it, we will submit a new payment request taking into account what you have already paid. Here there are no convoluted procedures and hidden charges.

Dedicated server

Enterprise level services

The dedicated server is ideal when you need maximum confidentiality and you are a group of multiple users. It is logically more expensive than a simple account on a shared infrastructure, here you are the only beneficiary of the hardware resources of our machines.

Tailored offers

For this type of service we do not make offers on the fly, send us an quote request from the dedicated page and please answer the following questions:

  • How many users will be using the server?
  • What volume of storage (to be shared between users) is needed?
  • Are you interested in the Collabora Online service?

    We are looking forward to your message, we will then return to you with a proposal and a detailed quote.
