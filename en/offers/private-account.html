Nextcloud

Nextcloud is a complete online service to replace Dropbox and the Google suite (Drive, Docs, etc). You upload your files there and then you can synchronize between your various devices with dedicated applications. Nextcloud can manage your files and directories, calendars, RSS feeds, bookmarks of your browser and you can even do video conferencing!

You must choose between two offers:

  1. The option of encrypting your data on our server, the administration team cannot read the contents of these last but also can not recover them if you lose your password!
  2. Access to the office suite Collabora Online (CODE), to collaboratively edit your office documents (text, calc and presentations).
