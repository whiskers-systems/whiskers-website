Nextcloud

Nextcloud is a complete online service to replace Dropbox and the Google suite (Drive, Docs, etc). You will be able to upload your files, which you can then synchronize between your various devices with the dedicated applications. Nextcloud can manage your files and directories, calendars, RSS feeds, bookmarks of your browser and you can even do video conferencing!

GitLab

GitLab Community Edition is a free software forge for managing the development stages of software from A to Z. Enjoy unlimited repositories, fine-tune access to them and track tickets and reports. Bug while documenting your project in the built-in wiki. You can even set up the construction process, test, verification of your software with GitLab-CI.

Suite e-mail Mailcow

Mailcow is a full email suite based on famous software Postfix, Dovecot, Spamassassin, ClamAV, OpenDKIM and the Roundcube webmail. The best of e-mail on your own server, the ideal solution to reappropriate your data. Access is secured by TLS with certificates Let's Encrypt.
