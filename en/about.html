About

Because trust is not an option, here is what we can say about us.

Welcome by Whiskers Systems

Whiskers Systems is a company created by Mathias, IT guy by profession, privacy advocate and new technology enthusiast.

Microbusiness

Whiskers Systems is a company affected by the Peter Pan syndrome! We do not want to grow, we won't sell you 10 000 revolutionary services. Here we concentrate on what we do best, to offer you the perfect hosting solution who fits your needs.

Close to you

We are actually based on France but we regulary move through the whole Europe. Nos machines sont hébergées à Londres, Francfort et Amsterdam. Nous croyons en un monde sans frontières, pour vous fournir rapidement des offres adaptées et performantes, où que vous soyez.

Third Party Services

  • Our servers and network are provided by the New York company DigitalOcean.
  • We buy our domain names in the French company Gandi.
  • The public monitoring page comes from the Parisian company Updown.io.
  • The payment is managed by the Parisian company PayPlug.
  • The small online chat system is by Chatkwik and pass through Slack
  • Emails contact forms are transmitted by Formspree.

Free Software

Whiskers Systems is committed to Free software, for us the Free is not a selling point but a real investment. We are members of the April since our creation, also find our software repositories on GitHub.
We use only Free Software for our infrastructure, among many others you can cross the universal operating system Debian, the containerization system Docker, the webserver Nginx, language PHP or Perl, the database system MariaDB.
